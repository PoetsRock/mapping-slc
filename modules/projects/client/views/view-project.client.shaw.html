Andrew Shaw is a songwriter, composer, and performer based in Salt Lake City. He composed, performed, and recorded the original score for the feature films The Strongest Man (Sundance Film Festival 2015) and Must Come Down. His music has been used in public television and commercial applications, as well.

Shaw writes and performs music with a handful of bands and solo acts, including Color Animal, Magic Mint, Albino Father, and Andrew Shaw. He has previously performed as The Platte, was a member of Calico and Bluebird Radio, and has sat in with David Williams, Will Sartain, Band of Annuals, GIANT, and many others.
